## Status Vocabulary

Working-docs classification uses only:

- `done`
- `partial`
- `blocking`
- `deferred`
- `planned`
- `unknown / needs evidence`

CWQ execution status may later use the execution brief values:

- `pending`
- `in_progress`
- `worker_done`
- `qa_failed`
- `repairing`
- `qa_passed`
- `blocked_external`
- `waived_p1`
- `closed`

C0 may be marked `qa_passed` after independent QA. Implementation packets
remain open until their own worker/QA gates pass.
